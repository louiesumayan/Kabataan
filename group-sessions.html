Group Sessions

We're Kabataan, a mental health builder for Filipino youth, and we're inviting you to join our Mental Health Group Sessions activity. In these sessions, we provide a safe and supportive space for young people to share their experiences and learn from each other. We believe this is an essential step in promoting mental health and well-being among our youth.

Features

So if you're interested in joining us, please sign up for our next session! We promise it will be a worthwhile experience for you.

Connect With Others

The sessions are an opportunity for participants to connect with others going through similar experiences and learn from each other.

Mental Health Professionals

The sessions are led by mental health professionals who are experienced in working with young people.

Understand Mental Health

The sessions are designed to help participants learn about and understand their own mental health and how to support their peers.

Confidential

The sessions are confidential, and participants are free to share as much or as little as they feel comfortable with.

Supportive and Non-Judgemental

The sessions are supportive and non-judgmental, and participants are encouraged to express themselves in whatever way they feel comfortable.

Safe and Welcoming Environment

The sessions are held in a safe and welcoming environment, where everyone is respected and valued.

Contact Us

If you're looking for a way to improve your mental health, consider joining Kabataan's Mental Health Group Sessions. This activity is designed to help Filipino youth build mental resilience and cope with stress.

You'll learn how to manage your emotions and thoughts through group discussions and activities. You'll also have the opportunity to share your experiences with others going through similar challenges.
